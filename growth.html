LaunchPad Growth Fund

WEBINAR REPLAY & HIGHLIGHTS

In October, we hosted an exclusive webinar introducing the LaunchPad Growth Fund, our high-yield, secured real estate strategy focused on the fast-growing San Bernardino market.

During the session, we detailed how the Fund is structured to deliver fixed 20–30% annual returns, secured by a first-lien position across multiple land acquisitions—providing strong protection and meaningful upside to investors.

We also covered why this is a straight debt investment with no fees or carried interest, how the entitlement phase offers the highest return for the lowest risk, and how our projects align with the City's priorities around mixed-use development and new housing.
