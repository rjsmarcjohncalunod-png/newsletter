New: Fireside Chat Series

WITH LAUNCHPAD'S COMMUNITY PARTNERS

We're excited to launch our new Fireside Chat Series, featuring conversations with the community partners who share and strengthen our mission. Our featured partners include S2A Modular, With Open Arms, Asociación de Emprendedores, Latinos Transformando Latinos, LiUNA, Stokes Foundation, TKO Financial Network, San Bernardino Community College District, and RevGen Consulting.

In this series, these leaders reflect on the pivotal moments that shaped their journeys, the purpose that drives their work, and why they partnered with LaunchPad to support lasting, positive change in San Bernardino.

We invite you to read the Full Q&A on our website and watch their interviews on our YouTube channel - their stories are powerful.
