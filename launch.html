DEL ROSA LAUNCHPAD

INVESTOR UPDATE

Our Del Rosa LaunchPad continues to advance on schedule and is entering a critical phase of design, alignment, and value creation. Over the past quarter, we completed key steps in the site plan, advanced modular coordination with S2A, and strengthened our working relationship with the City's Planning Department — de-risking the entitlement path.

This trajectory reduces entitlement risk, increases underlying land value, and reinforces Del Rosa as the flagship proof of concept for the LaunchPad model — integrating housing, small business spaces, and workforce opportunities into one scalable community.
